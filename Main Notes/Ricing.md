[[Productivity]]
2026-07-20 16:45

I have a love hate relationship with ricing. On one hand, you get to configure the ultimate system with all your preferences, productivity setups, and develop a cohesive, aesthetic daily experience. On the other hand, it sucks away your time, often requires heavy maintenance, and you often end up redoing it. This is why I'm moving toward a more minimal approach that keeps my tooling modular, stores away my favorite keybindings, retains my wallpapers between configurations, and ultimately should help save me from feeling trapped on any given setup. 

The following are modular components that I feel should be portable and moved between any given setup.
- Wallpapers
- Notes & Study Setup
- Browser extensions
- Dev Setup
- Keybindings
