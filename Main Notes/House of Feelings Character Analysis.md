## Season 3

### Wintr
- A lot of dating and relationship experience vastly helps your ability to understand other people. You have a greater grasp of emotions and you have a better heuristic for the things you should and shouldn't do. He clearly has both a degree of natural talent and gathered experience that help with his game.
- As seen in his first date with Ray, he employs numerous strategies to attract her attention
	- He's immediately very outgoing, confident, compliments her politely, and smiles brightly
	- He says the same first ideal date as her, a calming but active hike (many people enjoy a light activity followed by a dinner to get to know each other on a deeper level)
	- He correctly guesses a lot of things about her like the traits of her type, playing on her interest in him by describing his own best traits
	- He balances everything, avoiding being clingy, overly self-centered, and appearing confident, being slightly hard to get at. He makes people feel secure by being understanding, while also questioning things like her favorite dish
- Being charismatic is a combination of countless factors, but being positive and playful is a hugely important trait that shouldn't be overlooked. When you have an uplifting energy, it not only helps with your outlook on life, but it also helps you have a healthier relationship with others and attract others who share this energy. 
- Wintr, although being highly playful, displays his ability to be empathetic and understand relationship dynamics. He describes to Ashley through an analogy how nobody will ever be perfect, glasses aren't ever full to the top, but all it takes is her or someone else to fill up the rest (working together to build a stronger relationship together and become better for each other). He is considerate of others, identifying Ray's boundaries and being highly playful once he sees some reciprocation.

### Mack
- Softer personality that makes everyone feel comfortable. He's very secure and honest, empathizing with others and simply trying to get to know others better. In terms of relationships, prioritizing yourself while trying to care for others strikes a healthy balance.
- He joked a lot about his personal shortcomings, making light of them and creating a humorous effect liked by others. His positive outlook definitely is an important thing everyone should strive for.
- To create attraction, interest, and avoiding being friendzoned, Mack could do a few things
	- Express himself more romantically and being straightforward with his intentions. Mack being a people pleaser is afraid to cross boundaries and prioritizes making others feel comfortable. Part of being masculine is being decisive, and by never clearly asserting any romatic intentions, girls see him a more brotherly manner. Although making others feel comfortable and reassuring them, avoiding crossing any boundaries is a highly valuable trait, making moves subtly and in a way that doesn't cause discomfort is critical to allow deeper feelings of attraction to be developed. Wintr clearly compliments Ray, expresses how she makes him feel, is playful, does gentlemanly things while comforting her and trying to get to know her better strikes a sweet spot.

### Richard
- He stands out from the rest of the cast by his reserved nature, clear sense of direction, maturity and wisdom, along with his calming presence. Particularly in the beginning of the show, he doesn't like the party feeling, and he expresses how he thinks dating for fun is pointless. Rather, he explains how he dates to marry and learn truths about himself and gain a greater understanding of how to navigate relationships to ultimately have a fulfilling and stable connection with his spouse.
- His perspectives are very grounded, and he seems like a stable man that would make women feel comfortable. His personality gravitates toward people similar to him, who care about family, and are down to earth. Initially, he makes Ashley feel uncomfortable with his physical touch, likely stemming from his protective instinct. Ultimately he was mature and cleared things up as soon as he learned that he had made her feel this way. I think his story shows how dating can serve as a great early compatibility and understanding filter, enabling you to better get to understand another person without hurting their feelings in any significant manner. Him and Ashley clearly had differences, and they easily sorted it out and moved on without any haste. His discussions with Ayako also immediately revealed their shared values and similarly reserved personalities.

###  Michael
- Being fully honest, it becomes immediately honest that there is a lack of emotional intelligence and maturity. Deep down, he doesn't mean to cause others discomfort and just wants to have fun, but he inadvertently makes jokes or does things he shouldn't. In his situation, he will simply have to learn through experience to develop as a person.
- Mack and Michael develop a friendship, likely because Michael often feels alienated by others when they pick up on his unnatural tendencies, but Mack, being an understanding person makes him feel understood. Mack sees him for his good side and is always friendly with him, creating a unique bond between them.
- His offhand remarks about Ashley and Ayako despite his clear attachment to Hena and his general behavior show his tendency to use deflective humor when he feels uncomfortable. Rather than having deep interactions and potentially uncomfortable discussions, he resorts to superficial joking to avoid confronting the difficulties that come with commitment.

### Jay
- Everyone has their own story and unresolved conflicts from the past. Jay is a prime example of someone who isn't fully content with who they are and was hurt through past traumatic experiences through relationships. He has a very tough demeanor, but he has a softer side that comes out around people he feels comfortable with. Throughout the course of the show, it becomes clear that his story is a complicated one, which also helps him connect at a deeper level with the rest of the house.
- His highly masculine energy with a touch of vulnerability is attractive, although he occasionally comes off as a bit goofy, that side of him is seen as cute. Although he occasionally trips up and is too straightforward, he is honest and seeking personal growth, which are highly valuable traits.

## Season 2

### Prosper ([[Boyfriend Maxxing]])
- Initially, he has good chemistry with both Nara and Sara, displaying his affectionate and caring side, while being flirtatious. Ultimately what gives him trouble throughout the show is his cringey and premeditated flirting attempts that feel fabricated. Later he also struggles with his avoidant nature, recognizing some of his toxic tendencies hoping to grow as an individual.
- Later in the show, he accepts his shortcomings, hoping to continue using it as an opportunity to further learn about himself and grow from his mistakes.
- His use of eye contact, consideration (like putting his jacket around Nara and hugging her when she says she feels cold), and flirty behavior creates tension, building anticipation and indirectly showing his interest.

### Viet
- From a personal development and faith standpoint, Viet has gone through tough experiences, growing up with an abusive father and running away when he was 17. He learned from these traumatic experiences, choosing to remember, and embracing vulnerability to blossom by healing internally. He is articulate and wise with his words, revealing the layers to his character as an individual under his tough surface.
