Analogies bridge unfamiliar concepts with familiar ones, clarifying complex ideas. Explicitly mapping shared features between the target and analogue helps build connections while also acknowledging where the comparison breaks down is critical to an effective analogy.

### Relationships
- It isn't always the best time to be buying new shares and investing, but it's also those times that are the worst to panic sell them. Relationships have those same ups and downs, but the real question is if you believe in the company.
- If you are on a basketball team together, there may be a game where your team member isn't scoring as much, but you can't blame the loss on them since there are days where they make up for your deficiencies. They may have made other contributions or even just had something else going on in life. You lost as a team, and you need to support each other to grow stronger.
