Using social media as a medium for marketing can be far more effective for greater engagement than standard advertisements. Telling the story of your product through a compelling narrative that doesn't feel like its asking for the user's money can create a more loyal user-base and promote sales.

Following standard storytelling techniques is important
- Apply a setup, conflict, and resolution structure to short-form content to tell your story
- Establish an engaging hook within the first 3 seconds
- Show vulnerability and finish with a strong call-to-action
