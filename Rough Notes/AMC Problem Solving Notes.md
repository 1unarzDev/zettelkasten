- So long as the constant multiplying each term in a geometric series is the same between alternating signs, just make the ratio negative rather than splitting it into two series.
- Avoid solving quadratics by arranging into a complete the square setup.
- Minimize variables in more complex problems by defining them in terms of another variable whenever possible.
