### Key Takeaways
- Your values, underlying principles you prioritize, shape who you are. You only have so many fucks to give, so the things you choose to care about are critical.
- The metrics you evaluate yourself on how well you are aligning with your values. A good metric gives you feedback in ways that reinforce beneficial behaviors and punish negative behaviors. A poor metric gives you a false direction.
- Stop thinking about all the things you want or could achieve. Ask yourself what pain you're willing to endure. Humans are inherently wired to feel pain and seek problems to promote positive adaptations. You will never eliminate a problem, just lessen it.
